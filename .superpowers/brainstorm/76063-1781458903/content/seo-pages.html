How SEO landing pages work differently from your existing content

Your existing pages (deep-dive notes)
  • Written for people already on the site
  • Deep, comprehensive, long-form
  • Not keyword-targeted
  • Google doesn't know what query they answer
SEO landing pages (new)
  • Built around one specific search query
  • Answer that query in the first 200 words
  • Then link deep into your existing content
  • Google indexes them for that exact query
