## Why I Built VamsiLabs

After preparing for interviews at multiple product companies, I realized most online resources are either:

- **Too shallow** — generic definitions copy-pasted from documentation
- **Too scattered** — you need 15 tabs open to study one topic
- **Not from practitioners** — written by content writers, not engineers who've built these systems

VamsiLabs is the resource I wish I had — **300+ deep-dive articles** covering Java internals, Spring Boot production patterns, and system design, all written from the perspective of someone who's actually built and operated these systems at scale.
